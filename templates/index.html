{% block title %} Welcome to RTcare {% endblock %}

Currently only digital area working. You can only manage BSI security cards at the moment. More coming soon!

What does it do?

RTcare lets you manage all the things needed for participation on the german BOSnet. It also lets you manage all your Radio Terminals, no matter them being analog or digital and no matter what type (HRT, MRT, FRT) they are. This software is very flexible and meant to be used instead of some big and ugly Excel-Sheet

Where do I start?

First you select the correct area at the top. If you want to manage your digital radio terminals please select digital, otherwise select analog
